Hitman III

Age Rating: 18+

PS4
Xbox
PC

Year of Release: 2019

Hitman is a thrilling stealth action game that follows the story of Agent 47, a highly skilled contract killer. The game's innovative design allows players to take down targets in a myriad of ways, offering an incredible amount of freedom and creativity. With diverse locales ranging from bustling city streets to isolated mountain retreats, each mission presents a unique and complex sandbox filled with potential strategies and paths. Players must use their wits, environment, and a wide assortment of tools to plan and execute their assassinations, making each successful hit a satisfying and rewarding experience. Although the game includes violent scenarios, it equally emphasizes strategic thinking, patience, and precision. With its compelling story, deep gameplay mechanics, and high replay value, Hitman is a must-play for fans of the stealth-action genre.
