Synopsis

"Riverdale" is an American television series based on the characters of Archie Comics. Set in the small town of Riverdale, the story begins after the mysterious death of high school golden boy Jason Blossom. This prompts a closer look at the town's image of perfect suburbia, unearthing its undercurrent of secrets and crimes. The story focuses on Archie Andrews, Betty Cooper, Veronica Lodge, and Jughead Jones, each representing a different aspect of typical American teen life. As they navigate high school and deal with the trials of youth, they encounter more than their fair share of mystery and drama, while also dealing with issues of love, friendship, and identity.
