About Warner Bros

Who are we

Warner Bros. Entertainment Inc., a subsidiary of WarnerMedia, is a premier global leader in the entertainment industry. The company was founded on April 4, 1923, by brothers Harry, Albert, Sam, and Jack Warner. Over nearly a century of operation, Warner Bros. has been at the forefront of the film and television industries, producing an impressive array of iconic and impactful content. The studio has been responsible for some of the most successful and beloved films and television shows in the history of visual storytelling, including classics like "Casablanca," "The Wizard of Oz, " the "Harry Potter" series, and the "Dark Knight" trilogy.Warner Bros. is renowned not only for its vast and diverse catalogue of feature films, television shows, and animated content, but also for its pioneering efforts in the realm of digital distribution and emerging technologies. It is committed to maximizing the potential of its creative talents and providing high-quality, compelling entertainment to audiences around the globe. Beyond its film and television production, Warner Bros. has diverse business segments including home entertainment, global distribution, and interactive entertainment, offering integrated, coordinated strategies and solutions across the company.
